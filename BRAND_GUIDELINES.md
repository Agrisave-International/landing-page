# AGRISAVE Brand Guidelines

## Brand Colors

### Primary Colors
```css
Primary Green:   #2D5016  /* Dark green - headers, emphasis */
Secondary Green: #4A7C25  /* Brand green - buttons, links, icons */
Accent Green:    #5FA832  /* Bright green - highlights */
Light Green:     #E8F5E0  /* Backgrounds, subtle accents */
White:           #FFFFFF  /* Primary background, text on dark */
```

### Supporting Colors
```css
Charcoal:        #2C2C2C  /* Body text, footer background */
Gray Medium:     #999999  /* Secondary text */
Gray Light:      #E5E5E5  /* Borders, dividers */
```

## Color Usage

### Backgrounds
- **Hero Section**: White to light green gradient
- **Main Sections**: White
- **Alternating Sections**: Light green (#E8F5E0)
- **Technology Section**: Green gradient (primary to secondary)
- **Footer**: Charcoal (#2C2C2C)

### Text
- **Headers**: Primary green (#2D5016)
- **Body Text**: Charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- **Links**: Secondary green (#4A7C25)
- **On Dark Backgrounds**: White (#FFFFFF)

### Interactive Elements
- **Primary Buttons**: Secondary green (#4A7C25)
- **Button Hover**: Primary green (#2D5016)
- **Icons**: Secondary green (#4A7C25)
- **Borders**: Light green (#E8F5E0)

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Fallback**: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif

### Font Weights
- **Headers**: 700-800 (Bold to Extra Bold)
- **Subheaders**: 600 (Semi Bold)
- **Body**: 400-500 (Regular to Medium)
- **Emphasis**: 600 (Semi Bold)

### Font Sizes
```css
H1: 3.5rem (56px)   - Hero headlines
H2: 2.5rem (40px)   - Section headers
H3: 1.5rem (24px)   - Card titles
Body: 1rem (16px)   - Regular text
Small: 0.9rem (14px) - Fine print
```

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- **File**: `agrisave-logo.png`
- **Design**: Letter "A" with green leaves and brown soil
- **Colors**: Green (#4A7C25) and brown
- **Background**: Transparent

### Logo Placement
- **Navigation**: Logo + "AGRISAVE" text
- **Footer**: "AGRISAVE" text only (no logo)
- **Favicon**: Logo as site icon
- **Minimum Size**: 32px height
- **Clear Space**: Equal to logo height on all sides

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't change logo colors
- ❌ Don't distort or stretch
- ❌ Don't add effects or shadows
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds

## Icons

### Style
- **Type**: Line icons (Feather Icons style)
- **Stroke Width**: 2px
- **Color**: Secondary green (#4A7C25)
- **Size**: 48px for features, 64px for testimonials

### Icon Usage
- Problem section: Eye-off, Dollar, Clock
- Feature section: Satellite, Water droplet, Bar chart, Mobile
- Testimonials: User profile icons
- Social media: Brand-specific icons

### Style

- **Border**: 2px solid light green (#E8F5E0)

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional yet approachable
- Innovative yet grounded
- Tech-forward yet farmer-friendly
- Confident but not arrogant

### Key Messages
- "Your Farm. From Space. Powered by AI."
- "Monitor crop health, detect problems 2 weeks early"
- "Professional farm monitoring for $29/month"
- "Made in the US with ❤️ for farmers worldwide"

### Language Guidelines
- ✅ Use "you" and "your" (direct address)
- ✅ Speak farmer-to-farmer
- ✅ Explain technical concepts simply
- ✅ Use concrete examples and numbers
- ❌ Avoid excessive jargon
- ❌ Don't talk down to farmers
- ❌ Don't overhype or exaggerate

## Imagery

### Style
- High-quality farm photography
- Real farmers (diverse representation)
- Modern technology + traditional farming
- Natural lighting preferred
- Professional but authentic

### Colors in Images
- Emphasize green (crops, fields)
- Natural earth tones
- Avoid overly saturated colors
- Maintain brand color harmony

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- **Text on White**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Text on Green**: Use white text
- **Links**: Underline on hover
- **Focus States**: Visible outline

### Best Practices
- Alt text for all images
- Keyboard navigation support
- ARIA labels where needed
- Semantic HTML structure

## Brand Tagline

**Primary**: "Empowering Farmers Through Satellite Intelligence"

**Alternatives**:
- "Monitor Your Farm From Space"
- "AI-Powered Precision Agriculture"
- "Professional Farm Monitoring Made Simple"

## Footer Message

**Official**: "Made in the US with ❤️ for farmers worldwide"

This emphasizes:
- US-based company (trust & quality)
- Global reach (worldwide farmers)
- Passion for agriculture (heart)

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Keep it clean and simple
- Use plenty of white space
- Maintain consistent green theme
- Show real farmers and farms
- Be transparent about pricing
- Focus on benefits, not features

### ❌ Don't
- Use gold, blue, or brown accents
- Clutter the design
- Use stock photos that look fake
- Hide pricing or costs
- Use technical jargon without explanation
- Make unrealistic promises
